About Me

My Story

Hey! I’m Alex, the founder of Kallos Sthenos. What started as a few pull-ups in my backyard became a mission to help others unlock strength and mobility without any expensive gym gear.

After recovering from an injury that sidelined me from weightlifting, I turned to bodyweight exercises. Over time, I rebuilt not only my body—but my confidence and discipline. Calisthenics gave me freedom. Now, I help others discover the same.

My Mission

I believe anyone can master their body. My goal is to provide clear, achievable steps that make bodyweight fitness accessible for everyone—whether you're a total beginner or chasing advanced skills.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Step-by-step tutorials: From push-ups to muscle-ups.
  • Programs: Structured routines for all levels.
  • Mobility & recovery: Stay injury-free and fluid.
  • Community & coaching: Connect, share, and grow.

Who This Is For

Whether you're new to fitness or looking to master skills like the front lever or planche, this space is for you. No fancy gym. No machines. Just your body and the will to train smart.
